Day 2: Grammar Basics

Main Goal: Understand basic grammar so you can form correct English sentences while speaking.

üìò What is Grammar?

Grammar is the set of rules that tells us how words are arranged to make correct sentences. Without grammar, sentences may sound confusing or incorrect.

Good spoken English does not require complex grammar. You only need basic grammar + regular practice.

SubjectWho does the actionI, You, He, She
VerbAction wordeat, go, read
ObjectReceives the actionfood, book, school

The most common sentence pattern is:

Subject + Verb + Object

  • I eat food.
  • She reads a book.
  • They play cricket.
